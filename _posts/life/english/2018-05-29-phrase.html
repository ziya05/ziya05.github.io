--- layout: post title: 单词系列：短语 description: 逆流而上 categories: [life, english] slug: phrase gitment: true title_color: '#77CC00' ---

  • beat on, 扑向，奋力前行
  • boats against the current, 逆流而上
  • bear back, 倒回， 退回

例如： So we beat on, boats against the current, borne back ceaselessly(不停地) into the past. 于是我们奋力地逆水行舟，又注定要不停地退回。
